LOOPS & MODES

  • When the user is hovering over the card, the card flips over and reveals more menu information on how the image can be interacted with, such as writing a review, favoriting, and adding to a list. It also reveals statistics from other users from a database. When the user hovers off, the card flips back to the film poster and the user's choices are recorded in a database
